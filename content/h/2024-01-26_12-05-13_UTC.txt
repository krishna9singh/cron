South Korea has unveiled plans to introduce a new visa specifically for fans of South Korean culture. The Hallyu visa, also known as the 'K-culture training visa, will allow foreign individuals enrolled in local performing arts academies to stay in the country for up to two years. While details of the K-culture visa requirements are yet to be disclosed, further n information is expected to be announced in the second half of 2024.
