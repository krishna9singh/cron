Maa Kaalratri🖤🤍Kalaratri means the One who is “the Death of Kaal”. Here Kaal is referred to as death. Kalaratri is the one who destroys ignorance and removes darkness. This form primarily depicts that life also has dark side – the violent Mother Nature who nurtures life can also create havoc and remove all dirt. The Goddes is also known as Shubhankari.
