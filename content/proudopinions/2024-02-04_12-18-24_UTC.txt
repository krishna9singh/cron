This is a mini food vlog of my personal favourite Amritsari Naan in all of Delhi. This local place serves crispy and buttery amritsari naans, cooked in a tandoor and filled with a tasty potato filling. They serve this with a combination of choley and a spicy chutney. Surprisingly the cost of this naan is very reasonable and it Tastes amazing. I had a great experience trying out the food of this place. Hope you guys enjoy the video!
